## The facts (✅ verified against fintechfestival.sg, 2026‑07‑16)
- **Run by:** Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) / GFTN. **Free**, and finalists join **"without equity dilution."**
- **Deadline:** **14 August 2026, 23:59 SGT** (= 11:59 AM SGT‑16h US Pacific — it's a *morning Aug 14* deadline in the US).
- **Format:** an *accelerator/pitch program*, **not** a build‑during‑the‑event hackathon and **not** revenue‑gated — it judges the **solution + team**.
- **Rewards:** up to 20 finalists, each **S$20,000** stipend + mentoring; **one winner per problem statement wins S$80,000**.
- **2026 dates:** finalists announced **September** · virtual programme **Sept–Nov** · in‑person **Nov 11–13** · **Demo Day Nov 18** · winners **Nov 19**.
- **Apply at:** https://forms.gftn.co/gfh2026
- **Teams:** form the team *before* registering; **max 10 members**; each person on one team only; **one proposal per team**.

## ⚠️ Eligibility (verbatim requirements — check each honestly)

## The pitch — Vantage as accessible, AI‑native wealth intelligence
**One‑liner:** *Vantage is an AI broadcast desk that turns intimidating market data into a live, spoken, multilingual experience — making market intelligence feel native to the generation that grew up on streaming and short‑form video.*

**The problem (their framing):** digitally‑native users (Gen Z / young millennials) are underserved by traditional wealth tools — dense terminals, jargon, English‑only, passive dashboards. They learn from video, voice, and interactivity, not spreadsheets.

**Vantage's solution:**
- An **animated AI anchor** that charts any stock, explains the move in plain language, and **reads it aloud on air** — market data as a broadcast, not a spreadsheet.
- **Zero‑setup** demo, **6 languages** (UI + spoken answers), voice control, and gamified learning — meeting young users where they are.
- **Natural‑language commands**: "chart NVDA and why it's moving," "take me to Robinhood," "write a report" — the desk reasons and acts.
- Exportable Word/PowerPoint/Excel briefs and a market calendar with on‑air reminders.

**Why it's AI‑native (not AI‑garnished):** the desk's answers, report‑writing, and command interpretation are all LLM‑driven (Gemini + others), and it can run fully on a local model. AI *is* the product surface, not a feature bolted on.

**Business model:** freemium — free demo tier; paid **Pro Desk / Trading Floor** plans via Stripe (already implemented). Clear path to revenue without gating the accessibility mission.
